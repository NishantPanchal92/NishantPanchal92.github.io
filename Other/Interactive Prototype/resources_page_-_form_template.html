Co-op Authorization Form

Required to be submitted for semester long internships (Including Fall, Spring and Summer semesters)

About

The Co-op Authorization form needs to be submitted by the student planning on undertaking full-time or part-time off campus work during

the course of a semester (Fall, Spring or Summer). To submit for authorization, please fill the form below and submit it for approval. The

approval process requires the Co-op form to be signed by the program advisor to whom this form will be submitted. Please remember

Co-op is approved for one semester at a time. As such the employment start and end dates must fall within the semester start and finish

dates. On approval of this form you are required to obtain the authorization of Kenneth Little from Graduate Co-Op office in the Division of

Professional Practice. Once the authorization is granted you are also required to enroll for the Co-op course for that semester.

Please note: International students must comply with OIE requirements.
