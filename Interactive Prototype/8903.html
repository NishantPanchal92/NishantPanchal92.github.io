Required to be submitted by CS track students before registering for a special problems class.

About

This form is for graduate students wishing to register for a special problems (8903) course. This is not the MS Project Form. Complete this in

consultation with the IC/CS faculty member who will be responsible for assigning your grade and then submit it for approval. You will

automatically receive an email when your permit is entered. Be sure to check the credit hours for proper registration amount. Variable courses

default is one hour credit. You must change the hours in OSCAR to the proper credit total at the time you register. NOTE: Each credit hour

represents AT LEAST 50-60 hours of work by the student.
